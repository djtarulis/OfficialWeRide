Sponsorships

Let me begin by thanking you in advance. Without generous sponsors our mission and goals can be difficult to reach.

We Ride is a non-for-profit 501C3 Association. We are dedicated to making Miracles happen for Riley Children of NWI. We Ride has a wide range of fund-raising events to help accomplish our goals.

We host Bingos, have bake sales, Texas Holdem Tournaments, and concerts. All of which take a lot of time and money to coordinate. Donations help us keep the cost at a minimum so that all of the proceeds get donated to Riley Children's Hospital annually, in June. I am humbly asking for a donation to help us cover the cost of hosting events. Please consider one of the donation amounts below. And thank you again for your consideration!
